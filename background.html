Background

The novel coronavirus(COVID-19), which causes acute respiratory disease in humans has already spread to many countries. And the World Health Organization declared COVID-19 as a pandemic. To overcome this new virus, we suggest more accurate and easy to use a new testing method. In Japan, 65,769 people have been infected before(as of 28, August 2020). Furthermore, the government declared a state of emergency. So come to think of it, SARS-CoV-2 has an impact on our health and economy. We need a more accurate way of test. Today in Japan, to test whether we have this virus or not, we mainly use the PCR method, and judgment is determined by checking the existence of the RNA of this virus. However, in this method, we canâ€™t separate between the RNA of an infectious virus and that of an uninfectious virus. This is because there is a chance that PCR reacts to the RNA of the uninfectious virus and amplifying it. To increase the accuracy of the test, we suggest a new testing method using a molecular robot. This method is based on the infection of SARS-CoV-2, so it can improve this problem of the PCR method.
